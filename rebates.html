Federal Programs

Canada-Wide Incentives

The federal government offers significant rebates to encourage home energy efficiency improvements.

Most Popular

Canada Greener Homes Grant

Natural Resources Canada
Up to $5,000 for eligible retrofits

Eligible Upgrades:

  • Heat Pumps: $2,500 - $5,000
  • Insulation: Up to $5,000
  • Windows/Doors: Up to $5,000
  • Air Sealing: Up to $1,000
  • Smart Thermostats: $50

Requirements:

  • Pre-retrofit EnerGuide evaluation required
  • Post-retrofit evaluation to verify improvements
  • Work must be done by qualified contractors
We handle both pre- and post-retrofit evaluations!

Canada Greener Homes Loan

Natural Resources Canada
Up to $40,000 interest-free loan

Loan Features:

  • 0% interest rate
  • Up to 10-year repayment term
  • Can be combined with grants
  • Covers labor and materials

Eligible For:

  • Same retrofits as the Grant program
  • EnerGuide evaluation required

Oil to Heat Pump Affordability Grant

Natural Resources Canada
Up to $10,000 for oil-heated homes

Eligibility:

  • Currently heating with oil
  • Household income under $250,000
  • Installing a cold-climate heat pump
  • Atlantic Canada, Quebec, or select areas

Includes:

  • $5,000 federal contribution
  • Additional provincial top-ups available

Provincial Programs

Rebates by Province

Many provinces offer additional incentives that can be stacked with federal programs.

Ontario Energy Rebates

Enbridge Gas Home Efficiency Rebate Plus

Up to $5,000

For Enbridge Gas customers upgrading insulation, air sealing, and heating systems.

  • Attic insulation: up to $1,500
  • Basement insulation: up to $1,500
  • Air sealing: up to $500
  • Smart thermostat: $75

Save on Energy Programs

Varies

Ontario electricity customers can access various efficiency programs through their local utility.

Combined with Federal: Ontario homeowners can access up to $10,000+ in total rebates

British Columbia Energy Rebates

CleanBC Better Homes & Home Renovation Rebate Program

Up to $11,000

BC's comprehensive rebate program for heat pumps and efficiency upgrades.

  • Air-source heat pump: $3,000 - $6,000
  • Heat pump water heater: $1,000
  • Insulation upgrades: up to $3,000
  • Window upgrades: up to $2,000

FortisBC & BC Hydro Rebates

Additional savings

Utility-specific rebates that can often be combined with CleanBC programs.

Combined with Federal: BC homeowners can access up to $16,000+ in total rebates

Quebec Energy Rebates

Rénoclimat

Up to $7,500

Quebec's main energy efficiency program offering substantial rebates.

  • Heat pump installation: up to $5,500
  • Insulation: up to $4,000
  • Air sealing: up to $1,000

Hydro-Québec Rebates

Varies

Additional rebates for Hydro-Québec customers on eligible equipment.

Combined with Federal: Quebec homeowners can access up to $12,500+ in total rebates

Alberta Energy Rebates

Home Energy Plan Rebates

Up to $8,000

Alberta's rebate program for comprehensive home energy improvements.

  • Cold climate heat pump: up to $4,500
  • Insulation upgrades: up to $2,500
  • Windows and doors: up to $1,000
Combined with Federal: Alberta homeowners can access up to $13,000+ in total rebates

Nova Scotia Energy Rebates

HomeWarming Program

Free upgrades

Income-qualified Nova Scotians may receive free energy upgrades including insulation and heat pumps.

Efficiency Nova Scotia Rebates

Up to $5,000

Rebates for heat pumps, insulation, and other efficiency improvements.

  • Mini-split heat pump: up to $1,500
  • Central heat pump: up to $3,000
  • Insulation: varies by project
Combined with Federal: Nova Scotia homeowners can access up to $15,000+ in total rebates

Our Expertise

Building Science at Our Core

Our team brings deep technical expertise in building science, HVAC engineering, and energy efficiency.

Building Envelope

Understanding how heat, air, and moisture move through your home is fundamental to improving efficiency. We analyze the complete building envelope—walls, roof, foundation, windows—to identify improvement opportunities.

HVAC Systems

From traditional furnaces to cutting-edge heat pumps, we understand heating and cooling systems inside and out. We size systems properly and ensure they integrate seamlessly with your home.

Moisture Management

Poor moisture control can lead to mold, rot, and indoor air quality issues. We consider humidity and ventilation in every recommendation to protect your home and health.

Cold Climate Expertise

Canada's harsh winters require specialized knowledge. We're experts in cold-climate heat pumps and understand how to keep homes comfortable even in extreme temperatures.

Energy Modeling

We use advanced energy modeling software to predict performance and savings accurately. No guesswork—just data-driven recommendations.

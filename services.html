Service 05

Thermography Inspections

Advanced infrared thermal imaging to detect hidden problems before they become costly failures.

Our certified thermographers use state-of-the-art infrared cameras to identify issues invisible to the naked eye. From electrical faults to insulation deficiencies, thermal imaging provides a non-invasive way to assess the condition of your building's critical systems.

Thermography Applications:

  • Electrical Panel Inspections – Detect loose connections, overloaded circuits, and failing components before they cause fires
  • Building Envelope Analysis – Find air leaks, missing insulation, and thermal bridges
  • Moisture Detection – Identify water intrusion and hidden leaks in walls, roofs, and foundations
  • HVAC System Evaluation – Assess ductwork performance and detect blockages
  • Mechanical Equipment – Monitor motors, bearings, pumps, and compressors for overheating
  • Steam System Analysis – Identify failed steam traps and insulation issues
  • Flat Roof Inspections – Locate trapped moisture without destructive testing

Preventative Maintenance Inspections

Comprehensive equipment inspections using advanced diagnostic technologies to prevent costly breakdowns and ensure safety.

Our preventative maintenance programs use thermography and visual inspections to provide a complete picture of your equipment's health. Identify problems early, plan maintenance proactively, and avoid unexpected downtime.

Inspection Services:

  • Electrical Panel Inspections – Thermal scanning of breakers, connections, and wiring to prevent electrical fires and outages
  • Steam Trap Surveys – Identify failed steam traps wasting energy and causing system inefficiencies
  • Insulation Assessments – Thermal imaging to detect degraded or missing pipe/equipment insulation
  • Mechanical Equipment Inspections – Motors, pumps, compressors, fans, and gearboxes
  • Detailed Reporting – Prioritized findings with repair recommendations and severity ratings

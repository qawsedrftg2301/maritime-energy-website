The Process

How It Works

Your journey to an energy-efficient, comfortable home is simple. We guide you through every step, from initial assessment to ongoing support.

1

Book an Energy Audit

Start your energy efficiency journey with a comprehensive home energy audit. Schedule online or call us—we'll find a time that works for you.

What to Expect:

  • Quick online booking (takes 2 minutes)
  • Flexible scheduling including weekends
  • Confirmation email with preparation tips
  • Reminder 24 hours before your appointment
Pro Tip: Have your last 12 months of energy bills ready—it helps us analyze your usage patterns.
2

Receive Your Energy Report

Our certified energy advisor conducts a thorough 3-4 hour assessment of your home, using advanced diagnostic tools to identify every opportunity for improvement.

Your Report Includes:

  • Thermal imaging results showing heat loss areas
  • Insulation assessment for all areas
  • HVAC system efficiency analysis
  • Prioritized list of recommended upgrades
  • Estimated costs and potential savings for each improvement
Delivered within 48 hours Comprehensive PDF report + follow-up consultation call
3

Get Heat Pump Recommendations

Based on your energy audit results, we provide personalized heat pump recommendations tailored to your home's specific needs and your budget.

Our Consultation Covers:

  • Heat loss calculations specific to your home
  • Best heat pump type for your situation
  • System sizing to ensure comfort in Canadian winters
  • Brand and model recommendations
  • Total project cost and expected ROI
No Pressure: We provide options and recommendations—the decision is always yours.
4

Professional Installation

Once you've made your decision, our certified installation team handles everything—from permits to final commissioning. Most installations are completed in 1-2 days.

Installation Day:

  • Team arrives on time with all equipment
  • Protection of your home (floors, walls, furniture)
  • Professional installation following manufacturer specs
  • All electrical and ductwork modifications included
  • System testing and commissioning
  • Complete cleanup—we leave your home spotless
  • Hands-on training on your new system
  • All warranty documentation provided
100% Satisfaction Guarantee If you're not completely satisfied, we'll make it right.
5

Ongoing Support

Our relationship doesn't end at installation. We provide continued support to ensure your system delivers maximum comfort and savings for years to come.

After Installation:

  • 30-day follow-up to ensure everything is perfect
  • Seasonal maintenance reminders
  • Priority service for any issues
  • Annual efficiency check-ins
  • Optional maintenance plans for worry-free operation
